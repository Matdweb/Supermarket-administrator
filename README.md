## Features ğŸš€

### Add New Product: (`enterProduct`)
Input details for a new product and seamlessly add it to the inventory. <br />
  <img alt='enter-Product' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/62caf5a5-f111-4982-91ef-f00891fcbdc9' width='500' />

 ### Delete Product by ID: (`removeProductById`)
  Remove a specific product from the inventory using its unique ID. <br />
  <img alt='remove-Product-By-Id' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/e3e63fba-2443-4402-97b6-78cf48a026bb' width='500' />

### Most Valuable Product: (`getProducOfHigherValue`)
  Identify and view the most valuable product based on sales data.

### Product Listing: (`sortProductByCode`)
  Display a comprehensive table with details of all products in the inventory. <br />
  <img alt='list-elements-image' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/a4b2da90-b4bb-40d2-9865-bdcc2f08cfcf' width='500' />

- **Low Stock Analysis:** (`amountOfProductsLowInStock`)
  Quickly identify products with low stock and print their detailed information.

- **Sales Statistics:**
  - (`how manyKgsHaveSoldInGeneral`)
    Track the total kilograms sold across all products.
    
  - (`how many kgs have been sold of the product`)
    View the quantity of kilograms sold for a specific product.
    
  - (`printProductsThatAreMoreExpensiveThan`)
    Print products that are more expensive than a given product.
  <img alt='print-Products-That-Are-More-Expensive-Than' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/57ebb7b0-b2be-4b0a-85b0-8063fedccc32' width='500' />
    
  - (`how manyUnitsHaveSoldOfProduct`)
    Track the number of units sold for a specific product.
    
  - (`inventoryCost`)
    Calculate the total cost of the inventory.
    
  - (`costOfSales`)
    Determine the cost incurred by the supermarket for sold products.
    
  - (`salesValue`)
    Calculate the total value of sales.
    
  - (`SuperMarketProfitBySales`)
    Assess the supermarket's profit generated through sales.
    
  - (`SalesChartOfThe5BestSellingProducts`)
    Visualize a chart displaying the fifteen best-selling products.
  <img alt='Sales-Chart-Of-The-5-Best-Selling-Products' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/1bb139ae-2c57-4d96-9c43-81be838b801d' width='500' />
    
  - (`SalesChartOfThe5LeastSoldProducts`)
    Visualize a chart displaying the fifteen least sold products.
  <img alt='Sales-Chart-Of-The-5-Least-Sold-Products' src='https://github.com/Matdweb/Supermarket-administrator/assets/110640534/579e2e62-f18a-4eac-9b32-5db270efbd95' width='500' />
    
  - (`averageSalePrice`)
    Gain insights into the average sales prices of all products.
